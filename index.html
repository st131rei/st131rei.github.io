My favorite coffee

I love coffee so much that I make it with my own hands almost every day.In this site, I would like to introduce three coffees that I recommend.

  1. Nicaragua French Roast

    2. This is first and foremost a clean coffee with a sweet, chestnut-like aftertaste.

  2. Ethiopian Mocha City Roast

    3. This is the best coffee bean without saying.Although it is generally considered to be characterized by its acidity, the deep roast gives it a chocolate-like flavor.

  3. Indonesia Mandarin French Roast

    4. Mandarin is a variety characterized by a heavy bitterness. This bean, which undergoes a traditional selection called Sumatra style, sometimes has a spicy flavor. It is a great bean to pair with sweetness, as an accompaniment to cigarettes, or as a latte.
